# Ideas

## Claude's Ideashop

**Starfield parallax background** — Add multiple sprite layers of small white dots (generated with `Image.Text("·", 1,1,1)` or tiny PNGs) at different Z depths, scrolling downward at different speeds each refresh tick. Closer layers move faster, distant ones barely move — classic parallax warp effect. No extra image files needed; pure script. Fits the Star Trek aesthetic and fills the black void around the breathing logo.
